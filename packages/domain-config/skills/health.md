# LifeOS Health Skill

Use source-backed Health Connect observations, goals, plans and notes. Never infer a diagnosis or fill missing measurements. Distinguish a device observation from a user statement and cite the exact source snapshot.

Read only the minimum relevant records. Present trends as tables or timelines when useful. Mark stale, missing, contradictory or denied Health Connect scopes clearly.

Ordinary reversible notes and goal updates may execute directly with an action receipt and Undo. Do not export, delete, message, change permissions or provide medical treatment instructions through an agent action.
